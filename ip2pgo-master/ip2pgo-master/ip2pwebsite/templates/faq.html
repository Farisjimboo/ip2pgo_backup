{% trans "How safe to trade the ETH / ERC20 tokens in iP2PGo ? " %}

{% trans "It is very safe. " %}

{% trans "The ETH and ERC20 token trades are conducted via the Ethereum distributed computing network, which iP2PGo cannot and do not control. " %}

{% trans "When a seller enters into a transaction with a buyer, the seller transfers ETH or ERC20 tokens directly to an Escrow Smart Contract on the Ethereum blockchain. This Escrow Smart Contract is unique and created specially only for that particular transaction. Once the buyer transfer the funds to seller’s bank account and seller confirming it on the iP2PGo App, the Escrow Smart Contract will release the ETH or ERC20 tokens to the buyer’s wallet. " %}

{% trans "If the trade is smooth, only the buyer and seller can access and interact with the Escrow Smart Contract. iP2PGo can only access ETH or ERC20 tokens held in the Escrow Smart Contract if explicit digital permission is given by the buyer or seller and only in the event of a trade dispute and with a limited function of either releasing the ETH to the buyer or sending it back to the seller." %}

{% trans "How do I set my offer price ? What is a floor and ceiling price?" %}

{% trans "iP2PGo users may post offers to buy or sell ETH or ERC20 tokens in exchange for local currencies using the marketplace. Those that post offers (buy or sell) are called ‘Makers’." %}

{% trans "All offers are based on good-till-cancel (“GTC”) basis. Makers will have to set their Offer Spread above/below the Spot ask/bid price by varying the spread scale. The bid and ask price is based on bittrex.com " %}

{% trans "Unless the offer is cancelled manually by the Maker itself, the offer will still be valid irregardless of the ETH or ERC20 token future price movements, this is because the Maker offer is based on Spread above a moving market price rather than a fixed absolute price. " %}

{% trans "As an option the Maker can also set additional floor (minimum) price (for sell offer) and a ceiling (maximum) price (for a buy offer). During the period where the Maker’s buy (bid) offer price of the ETH or ERC20 token increases above the ceiling price, the Maker’s buy offer will be suspended from the marketplace and will only be reinstated once his/her buy offer price drops again to and below the ceiling price (if not yet hit by a taker) and vice versa in the case for sell offer." %}
